About Me

Hi, I'm Simran Taj. I'm an enthusiastic and passionate fashion model with a knack for bringing creativity to life through self-directed photoshoots. While my professional background is in Information Science and Engineering, where I excel in debugging, quality software delivery, and tech innovation, my heart lies in the world of fashion and modeling.

Background: I have always been a lifelong learner, committed to leadership and pioneering innovations in every field I step into. My journey in modeling began as a hobby, but it quickly grew into a significant part of my life. I enjoy exploring new concepts and styles, often blending my technical expertise with my creative pursuits.

Experience: In addition to my work as an Intern at CODSOFT and RNS Institute of Technology, I have consistently developed my modeling portfolio. My engineering background has equipped me with a unique perspective on problem-solving and creativity, which I apply to my modeling projects.

Skills and Interests:

  • Modeling: Specializing in self-directed photoshoots, I have honed my skills in posing, styling, and conceptualizing unique visual narratives.
  • Photography: A passion for capturing the essence of fashion through the lens, with a strong understanding of composition, lighting, and editing.
  • Tech Savvy: Proficient in programming languages like Java, Python, C, and more, which aids in my approach to creative and technical challenges.
  • Creative Innovation: Blending my analytical and creative skills to pioneer new pathways in both tech and fashion.

Personal Philosophy: I believe in the power of collective success and personal growth. Whether it's through collaborating on a tech project or a modeling shoot, I strive to bring out the best in myself and those around me. Adaptability and innovation are my driving forces, helping me thrive in dynamic environments and pursue my passion for fashion modeling with vigor and enthusiasm.
